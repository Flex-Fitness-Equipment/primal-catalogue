/ About Primal Strength

Founded in 2016 by Steven Rinaldi, Primal set out to transform the gym floor — moving beyond rows of cardio into world-class strength and functional spaces. Today it's the UK's leading commercial strength brand, exporting to 25+ countries.

The range spans three series — Personal, Pro and Performance. This catalogue covers the Performance Series: commercial-grade pin-loaded and plate-loaded machines built for high-traffic floors.
